FORM BEHAVIOR
The public form prepares a ride-request summary. The visitor can text it to (917) 900-2068, copy it, or call Guardian. No medical or payment data should be entered.

BEFORE GOING LIVE
1. Confirm the business phone number: (917) 900-2068.
2. Add a business email if desired.
3. Review Privacy Notice and Terms with legal counsel because laws and business arrangements vary.
4. Confirm all advertised vehicle/service options are available through Guardian or its transportation partners.
5. Test the SMS link on a mobile phone.

IMPORTANT
This is non-emergency transportation. Call 911 for emergencies. A submitted request is not a confirmed booking.

Questions, answered

Know what to expect.

Is this an ambulance or emergency service?

No. Guardian coordinates non-emergency transportation. Call 911 for emergency medical assistance.

Is the ride confirmed after I complete the form?

No. Guardian reviews the request and contacts you to confirm availability, trip details and pricing.

Can wheelchair-accessible transportation be requested?

Yes. Wheelchair-accessible service can be requested and is subject to route, timing and provider availability.

Can a case manager or attorney submit a request?

Yes. The guided form supports private riders and professional referral sources.

Can I request a round trip?

Yes. Select round trip and include the appointment or expected return details.
